TITLE:
Cashly - Smart Expense Tracking with AI

DESCRIPTION:
An AI-powered expense tracking app that automatically scans and analyzes receipt images using Google Gemini 2.5 Flash. Built with Next.js 16, it features a CRED-inspired design with interactive data visualizations, automatic expense categorization across 9 categories, and real-time spending analytics.
